# Moovez Knowledge Base

## 1. Introduction
Moovez is an on-demand moving and delivery service that revolutionizes how people transport large and bulky items. Our platform connects customers with available movers and vehicles, ensuring efficient, reliable, and affordable solutions for moving and delivery needs.

### Key Features:
- **On-Demand Service**: Book movers and vehicles in just a few clicks.
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Monitor the progress of your job in real-time.
- **Transparent Pricing**: Clear pricing based on vehicle type, number of movers, and job duration.
- **Flexible Options**: Tailored solutions for individuals, businesses, and special requirements.

---

## 2. Services Overview

### 2.1 Job Types
We offer four main types of services:

#### 1. Delivery
- Delivery jobs are quick and easy, typically taking under an hour from pickup to drop-off. Expect 15-20 minutes for loading and unloading, plus 20-30 minutes of driving time between locations. An additional 30 minutes of travel time may be needed to reach the pickup address.
- Ideal for: 1-5 items. 
Typical examples include:
> Couch
> Couch and loveseat
> Cabinet
> BBQ
> Bed (if already disassembled; beds requiring disassembly should be booked as moving jobs)
- Common use cases: Marketplace purchases, curbside/in-store pickups, construction material deliveries, and more.

***If your job requires more time for loading/unloading, please book a “Moving” job instead.***

#### 2. Moving
- Moving jobs are meant for larger-scale jobs requiring more time, equipment, or multiple trips.
- When to book a moving job:
> If your load exceeds 5 items.
> If loading/unloading will take longer than 15-20 minutes.
> If items require assembly or disassembly.
- Moving jobs typically require multiple trips for loading and unloading, and work time can range from ***1 to 12 hours***, depending on the size of the job.

- Common use cases:
> Apartment, condo, and house moves.
> Office downsizing, relocation services, storage moves, dump runs, etc.

 - Moving multiple pieces of furniture that require extra time for loading/unloading, plus blanket wrapping and strapping down.Perfect for apartments, condos, homes, offices, or junk removal.
- Hourly pricing based on vehicle size and number of movers.
- Best for full moves or jobs lasting more than 1 hour.
- Suitable for small suff like couch, house furniture, quick jobs

#### 3. Movers
- Labor-only service without a truck.
- Great for rearranging furniture, loading/unloading trucks, or handling shipping containers.
- Movers jobs are for when you need extra muscle but don’t need a truck.
- Ideal for:
> Moving furniture around an apartment, condo, house, or office.
> Helping to load or unload a rented moving truck, storage pods, C-Can, etc.
***Important Note: Movers can visit multiple locations, but if you need a vehicle to transport items between locations, please submit a Moving job instead.***

#### 4. Business
- Customized solutions for commercial requirements.

## 3. Scheduling a Job

### 3.1 Steps to Schedule
1. **Choose Your Job Type**:
   - Select from Delivery, Moving, Movers, or Business based on your needs.
2. **Enter Job Details**:
   - Provide pickup and drop-off locations (including additional stops if needed).
   - Select the appropriate vehicle type and number of movers.
   - Upload pictures/videos of items for accuracy.
   - Choose your preferred date and time.
3. **Confirm Booking**:
   - Receive confirmation via email or text.
   - Matched with qualified movers based on availability.

### 3.2 Operating Hours
- **Standard Hours**: 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM (7 days a week).
- **Extended Hours**: Available outside standard hours but may have limited availability.

## 4. What We Can Move

### 4.1 Items We Handle
- Furniture, appliances, household goods, business equipment, and packages.

### 4.2 Restrictions
We do not move:

## 5. Customer Responsibilities

### 5.1 Presence During Jobs
- You do not need to be present at pickup or drop-off locations.
- For larger items, ensure someone is available to assist if only one mover is booked.

### 5.2 Disassembly/Assembly
- Movers bring basic tools for disassembly/assembly.
- Complicated tasks requiring special tools are outside our scope.
- Include this requirement when booking a Moving or Movers job.

### 5.3 Pre-Move Requirements
To ensure a smooth and efficient move, please complete the following tasks before our movers arrive:

#### Appliance Disconnection & Disassembly Clause
- Moovez does not disconnect appliances from power sources, including but not limited to electricity, gas, or water. It is the customer's sole responsibility to ensure that all appliances are properly disconnected and that water lines are turned off before the movers arrive.
- If a customer requests or allows Moovez movers to perform any disconnection, such action is undertaken at the customer’s own risk. Moovez, its employees, contractors, and affiliates shall not be held liable for any damage, leaks, malfunctions, or other issues arising from the disconnection or improper handling of appliances.
- Moovez is not responsible for disassembling appliances, doors, or other fixtures to facilitate a move. If disassembly is required, it is the customer's responsibility to complete this before the move.

#### Appliances & Utilities
 - (REQUIRED) Fridge & Freezer: Unplug before the move, empty all contents, and allow it to defrost. Wipe dry and drain any excess water.
- (REQUIRED) Stove & Oven: Unplug or disconnect from the power source. Ensure the gas line (if applicable) is turned off and properly capped.
- (REQUIRED) Dishwasher: Disconnect from water and drain any excess water. Secure hoses inside the unit.
- (REQUIRED) Washing Machine: Unplug and detach water hoses. Allow time for hoses to dry before moving. Secure the drum (if applicable).
- (REQUIRED) Dryer: Unplug from the wall and disconnect any gas connections (if applicable).

#### Furniture & Large Items
- (Recommended) Beds & Frames: Disassemble large bed frames, remove bedding, and secure any loose parts.
- (Recommended) Dressers & Wardrobes: Empty drawers and secure doors with tape or straps.
- (Recommended) Couches & Sofas: Remove cushions and wrap them separately if possible.
- (Recommended) Glass or Fragile Items: Detach glass shelves, mirrors, and table tops. Wrap them securely in padding or blankets.

#### Electronics
- (Recommended) TVs & Monitors: Unplug, remove wall mounts (if applicable), and secure cables in a labeled bag.
- (Recommended) Computers & Gaming Systems: Disconnect all cables, pack securely, and back up important files.
- (Recommended) Modems & Routers: Unplug and pack with corresponding cables in a labeled box.

#### Packing & Preparation
- (Recommended) Boxes: Ensure all items are packed in sealed, labeled boxes.
- (Recommended) Loose Items: Secure loose items like lamps, wall art, and décor in proper packing materials.
- (RESTRICTED) Hazardous Materials: Dispose of or properly store flammable liquids, propane tanks, or other restricted items.
- (RESTRICTED) Perishable Food: Consume or dispose of perishable food before moving day.

#### Outdoor & Miscellaneous
- (Recommended) BBQ & Grills: Clean, disconnect propane, and secure all loose parts.
- (Recommended) Lawn Equipment: Drain gasoline and oil from lawnmowers, trimmers, and other equipment.
- (Recommended) Bikes & Sports Equipment: Remove accessories and pack small parts separately.

## 12. Multi-Stop Delivery Pricing

### 12.1 Pricing Formula
#### If Stop Is Added
**Subtotal** = Service Fee + (Labour Cost × Number of Stops) + Base Fare + (Distance in KM × Per KM Rate)

#### If No Stop Is Added
**Subtotal** = Service Fee + Labour Cost + Base Fare + (Distance in KM × Per KM Rate)

#### Additional Movers
- If Number of Movers = 2  
  **Subtotal** = Subtotal + Two Mover Rate
- If Number of Movers = 3  
  **Subtotal** = Subtotal + Three Mover Rate

#### Total Charge
**Total Charge** = (Subtotal + 5% GST) - 10% Discount
